Design a premium fashion e-commerce storefront for a French-inspired menswear and accessories brand focused on new arrivals.

## Design Vision

Create a clean, sophisticated, minimalist retail experience inspired by contemporary French fashion brands such as A.P.C., Sandro, AMI Paris, Sézane Homme, and Maison Kitsuné.

The experience must feel premium, editorial, and fashion-forward while remaining highly usable and conversion-focused.

Avoid looking like a marketplace or discount-driven e-commerce site.

Focus on:

* Elegant typography
* Large product imagery
* Generous whitespace
* Smooth browsing experience
* Refined micro-interactions
* Luxury fashion aesthetic
* Mobile-first shopping journey

The visual hierarchy should emphasize products over interface elements.

---

## Brand Personality

* Modern Parisian
* Minimalist
* Premium but approachable
* Timeless rather than trendy
* Confident and understated
* Functional luxury

---

## Design System Constraints

### Typography

Use:

* Font Family: Noto Sans
* Base Font Size: 16px
* Base Weight: 400
* Base Line Height: 20px

Scale:

* xs: 10px
* sm: 12px
* md: 12.8px
* lg: 14px
* xl: 15px
* 2xl: 16px
* 3xl: 19.2px
* 4xl: 21px

Typography should feel editorial and luxurious.

Use larger spacing between headings and content.

Avoid excessive font weights.

---

### Colors

Use semantic tokens only.

Primary surfaces:

* surface.base
* surface.muted
* surface.raised
* surface.strong

Text hierarchy:

* text.primary
* text.secondary
* text.tertiary

Interactive states:

* text.inverse

Overall appearance:

* White dominant
* Black typography
* Subtle gray accents

Avoid colorful UI.

Products should provide visual color.

---

### Layout Principles

Use a spacious grid.

Desktop:

* 12-column layout
* Large margins
* Wide product cards

Tablet:

* 2–3 column product grid

Mobile:

* 2 column product grid
* Sticky actions

Use consistent spacing tokens only.

No custom spacing values.

## Interaction Design

Animations must feel premium and subtle.

Motion:

* 300ms duration
* Ease-out transitions

Use:

* Fade
* Scale
* Slide

Avoid:

* Bounce
* Elastic
* Excessive motion

---

## Accessibility

Must comply with WCAG 2.2 AA.

Requirements:

* Full keyboard navigation
* Visible focus indicators
* Accessible forms
* Screen reader labels
* Minimum contrast compliance
* Touch targets ≥44px

Never hide focus states.

---

## E-Commerce UX Optimization

Prioritize:

* Product imagery
* Fast browsing
* Low cognitive load
* High discoverability
* Smooth checkout path

Reduce unnecessary banners, popups, and promotional noise.

The interface should feel like a premium French fashion brand rather than a marketplace.
